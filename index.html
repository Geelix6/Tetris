How to play

- Figures called tetromino will constantly fall from the top of the field

- When you completely fill a line, it is deleted, and all the lines above fall into the vacant space

- When the tetromino has no place to fall on the field, you will lose

- You can change the level of the game. Higher level = More points! Try to score as much as you can!

Keyboard controls

Arrow Left - Moves tetromino one cell to the left

Arrow Right - Moves tetromino one cell to the right

Arrow Down - Moves tetromino one cell down

Arrow Up - Rotates tetromino

Space - Drops tetromino instantly

Key «−» - Decreases game level (Min - Level 1)

Key «=» - Increases game level (Max - Level 9)

Escape - Pauses the game

Touch controls

Swipe Left - Moves tetromino left following your finger

Swipe Right - Moves tetromino right following your finger

Swipe Down slowly - Moves tetromino down following your finger

Swipe Down quickly - Drops tetromino instantly

Tap - Rotates tetromino
